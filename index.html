About Emporium

Founded in Dubai in 2010, Emporium has established itself as a premier destination for luxury furniture in the UAE. We combine traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design to create pieces that stand the test of time.

Our showroom in Dubai features over 5,000 square feet of meticulously curated furniture collections from around the world, as well as our own exclusive designs.
